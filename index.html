About me

Hello, my name is André Dias and I am 23 years old. I was born in Lisbon, Portugal and I am half Portuguese half Spanish.

In 2023 I graduated from the Univerity of Lisbon and got my degree in Computer Science. When I earned my computer science degree, I built a strong foundation in algorithms, data structures, software development, and computational theory. I gained hands-on experience with programming languages like Java, and C++, and learned the importance of problem-solving, debugging, and optimizing code. Additionally, I developed skills in critical thinking, teamwork, and adapting to new technologies, which are essential for thriving in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Now, I'm living in Madrid and have been working as a Software Engineer ever since. In my experience as a software engineer, I've had the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world projects, enhancing my skills in writing efficient and scalable code. I've gained a deeper understanding of the software development lifecycle, from planning and designing to testing and deploying applications. Working in diverse teams has taught me effective collaboration, code reviews, and version control with tools like Git. This experience has also improved my problem-solving abilities, especially when addressing complex bugs or optimizing performance, and has solidified my ability to work in agile environments to meet project deadlines and client needs.

My passion is developing games though, as you can see, so I usually spend most of my free time doing it. I use Unity with C#. I'm constantly learning and improving my skills, and I would love to work professionally in the industry one day.

I consider myself to be hardworking, professional, thorough, ambitious, but most importantly, fun to be around.
